About Us

We know the future because we are building it, iTorho is a brand crafted from its heritage and Nguni meaning of ‘Pursuing an Opportunity’ in the Digital Age. We are a digitalization platform that prides itself in leading South Africa into the 4th industrial revolution. We aim to help small businesses digitalize their operations and take charge of automating business tools for increased income generating opportunities.

Our Logo, a Silhoutee of a Bull ‘El Toro’ looking up to an Orange Moon reflecting the light of the Sun as it sets. Symbolizes our view of the Past, Present and Future. The journey to prosperity is a bull chasing the sun.

The future of Africa is digital migration, which is why we provide digital transformation for SMMEs and freelancers.
